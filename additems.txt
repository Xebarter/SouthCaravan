About SouthCaravan

Who We Are

SouthCaravan is a leading B2B vendor management and procurement platform headquartered in Kampala, Uganda, designed to transform how businesses across Africa and the world connect, source, and trade with confidence.

Founded with a vision to bridge the gap in African supply chains and empower businesses to access reliable suppliers globally, SouthCaravan provides a secure, transparent, and efficient digital marketplace where buyers and suppliers can discover opportunities, manage procurement processes, and build long-term business relationships.

Our platform simplifies complex procurement workflows — from vendor discovery and RFQ management to contract lifecycle, performance tracking, and payment reconciliation — while embedding compliance, security, and sustainability at its core.

Mission
To empower African and global businesses with a world-class B2B procurement platform that drives efficiency, transparency, and sustainable growth through technology and trusted connections.

Vision
To become the most trusted digital infrastructure for cross-border B2B trade in Africa and beyond — enabling seamless, compliant, and responsible procurement that accelerates economic development and creates value for all participants.

Our Story

SouthCaravan was born in Kampala, Uganda, out of a deep understanding of the challenges faced by businesses operating in emerging markets: fragmented supplier networks, lengthy manual procurement processes, limited visibility into vendor reliability, currency and regulatory complexities, and the need for greater transparency in supply chains.

Recognizing these pain points, our founders — seasoned professionals with backgrounds in procurement, technology, logistics, and international trade — set out to build a platform that combines the best of global B2B technology with deep local market expertise.

Since our inception, SouthCaravan has grown rapidly, connecting thousands of buyers and suppliers across multiple African countries and international markets. We have facilitated millions of dollars in procurement transactions while maintaining an unwavering focus on data security, regulatory compliance, and ethical business practices.

What We Offer

SouthCaravan delivers a comprehensive suite of tools tailored for modern B2B procurement and vendor management:

• Intelligent Vendor Discovery & Matching  
  Advanced search, AI-powered recommendations, and detailed supplier profiles to help buyers find the right partners quickly.

• Request for Quotation (RFQ) & Tender Management  
  Streamlined creation, distribution, bidding, and evaluation of RFQs and tenders with automated workflows.

• Supplier Onboarding & Qualification  
  Robust due diligence tools, document verification, compliance screening, and performance rating systems.

• Contract Lifecycle Management  
  Digital contract creation, negotiation, approval, storage, and renewal reminders.

• Procurement Analytics & Insights  
  Real-time dashboards, spend analysis, supplier performance metrics, and procurement trend reports.

• Secure Communication & Collaboration  
  Built-in messaging, document sharing, and audit-ready interaction logs.

• Compliance & Risk Management Tools  
  Sanctions screening, KYC/KYS processes, audit trails, and regulatory reporting support.

• Sustainable & ESG Procurement Features  
  Tools to track supplier sustainability credentials and support responsible sourcing goals.

Our platform is accessible via web and mobile interfaces, with enterprise-grade security and scalability to support both growing SMEs and large multinational organizations.

Global Reach, Local Roots

While proudly Ugandan at heart, SouthCaravan serves businesses throughout East Africa, West Africa, Southern Africa, and increasingly in Europe, Asia, and the Middle East.
