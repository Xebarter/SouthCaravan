SouthCaravan Cookie Policy

Introduction / What Are Cookies?

SouthCaravan is a B2B vendor management and procurement platform headquartered in Kampala, Uganda, and operating internationally. We are committed to transparency in how we collect and use data to provide a secure, efficient, and personalized experience for businesses, buyers, suppliers, and procurement teams.

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit our website (southcaravan.com) and platform. Cookies and similar technologies (such as pixels, web beacons, and local storage) allow us to remember your actions and preferences over time, enabling core platform functionality, enhancing user experience, and helping us understand how our services are used.

We use cookies for several important reasons on SouthCaravan:
- To enable secure login and session management for registered business accounts
- To support procurement workflows such as posting RFQs, managing vendor lists, and tracking supply chain activities
- To analyze platform usage and improve our services
- To remember your preferences and settings

This Cookie Policy explains the types of cookies we use, their purposes, and how you can manage them. It forms part of our overall Privacy Policy. By continuing to use SouthCaravan, you consent to our use of cookies in accordance with this policy, unless you adjust your preferences.

We regularly review and update our cookie practices to ensure compliance with applicable data protection laws, including the Uganda Data Protection and Privacy Act, GDPR (for EU users), and other relevant international regulations.

Types/Categories of Cookies Used

SouthCaravan uses different categories of cookies to deliver and improve our B2B procurement and vendor management platform. We group cookies as follows:

1. Strictly Necessary (Essential) Cookies
These cookies are essential for the platform to function properly and cannot be disabled. They enable core features such as:
- Secure user authentication and login sessions
- Maintaining your shopping/RFQ cart and procurement workflows
- Security and fraud prevention
- Basic navigation between pages

These cookies do not store any personally identifiable information beyond what is strictly required for the service to operate. They are usually session-based and expire when you close your browser.

2. Functional / Preference Cookies
These cookies allow us to remember your choices and provide a more personalized experience. Examples include:
- Remembering your preferred language, currency (where applicable), or dashboard layout
- Saving your filter preferences for vendor searches or product categories
- Keeping you logged in for convenience during long procurement sessions (if you select “Remember me”)

3. Performance / Analytics Cookies
These cookies help us understand how visitors and registered users interact with SouthCaravan. They allow us to:
- Analyze platform usage, page views, and feature engagement
- Identify popular procurement tools and areas for improvement
- Measure the effectiveness of our B2B features and user journeys
- Optimize performance and fix technical issues

We use this data in aggregated and anonymized form to enhance the overall experience for all users.

4. Marketing / Targeting Cookies (where applicable)
These cookies may be used to deliver more relevant content, measure the success of marketing campaigns, or support limited retargeting for business audiences. On a primarily B2B platform like SouthCaravan, these are used sparingly and focus on professional relevance rather than consumer advertising.

We also use third-party cookies from trusted service providers (such as analytics tools) to support the above categories. You can manage your consent preferences at any time through our cookie settings banner or browser controls.

Detailed Cookie Inventory / List

Below is a detailed list of the main cookies used on the SouthCaravan platform. This list is based on a regular technical audit and may be updated from time to time. We distinguish between first-party cookies (set by SouthCaravan) and third-party cookies (set by our trusted service providers).

Cookie Name | Provider | Type/Category | Purpose | Duration
---|---|---|---|---
session_id / PHPSESSID | SouthCaravan (first-party) | Strictly Necessary | Maintains your active session and authentication status while using the platform | Session (expires when browser closes)
sc_auth_token | SouthCaravan (first-party) | Strictly Necessary | Secure authentication for logged-in business users and procurement workflows | 30 days (or until logout)
sc_language | SouthCaravan (first-party) | Functional / Preference | Remembers your selected language preference | 1 year
sc_filters | SouthCaravan (first-party) | Functional / Preference | Saves your search and filter preferences for vendors, RFQs, and products | 6 months
_ga | Google Analytics (third-party) | Performance / Analytics | Distinguishes unique users and sessions for aggregated usage analytics | 2 years
_ga_XXXXXX | Google Analytics (third-party) | Performance / Analytics | Tracks session-specific data for platform improvement | 2 years
sc_analytics | SouthCaravan (first-party) | Performance / Analytics | Internal analytics to understand feature usage and optimize B2B workflows | 1 year
sc_cart | SouthCaravan (first-party) | Strictly Necessary | Stores items in your RFQ or procurement cart | Session or 7 days
hubspotutk (if integrated) | HubSpot (third-party) | Performance / Analytics | Tracks user behavior for B2B lead intelligence and platform optimization | 6 months
__cfduid or similar | Cloudflare (third-party) | Strictly Necessary | Security and DDoS protection for reliable platform access | 30 days

Notes:
- “Session” duration means the cookie is deleted when you close your browser.
- Some cookies are only set for logged-in users or when specific features are used.
- We may use additional cookies for security, load balancing, or integrations with enterprise tools (e.g., ERP connectors). These are reviewed regularly.
- Third-party providers are contractually required to protect any data they process and use it only for the purposes outlined.

This table is not exhaustive. For the most current list, please check our cookie consent management tool or contact us. SouthCaravan does not use cookies for cross-site behavioral advertising targeted at individual consumers.

How Cookies Are Used on the Platform

- Maintain session continuity during complex workflows, such as multi-step vendor onboarding, product catalog browsing, or supply chain visibility tools, so users do not lose progress when navigating between pages.
- Remember user preferences, including language (English, French, or others), currency display settings, and dashboard customizations, making the platform more intuitive for our diverse international user base.
- Support analytics that help us understand how businesses interact with key features — for example, which supplier search filters are most used, how long users spend on the RFQ dashboard, or which integration tools (such as ERP connectors) are most valuable — so we can continuously improve the platform.
- Facilitate essential e-commerce functions like saving items in an RFQ cart or shortlisting vendors for future reference.
- Enhance security by detecting unusual activity and preventing unauthorized access to business data.

Because SouthCaravan primarily serves business users rather than individual consumers, our cookie usage focuses on operational efficiency, platform performance, and legitimate business interests rather than personalized consumer advertising. All cookie usage is designed to support transparent, efficient, and compliant B2B trade across borders.

Managing Cookies and Consent

You have several options to manage cookies on SouthCaravan:

1. Cookie Consent Banner
   When you first visit our platform, you will see a cookie consent banner. You can accept all cookies, reject non-essential cookies, or customize your preferences by category.

2. Cookie Settings Center
   Registered users can access a dedicated cookie settings page in their account dashboard to review and update their preferences at any time.

3. Browser Settings
   Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:
   - Block all cookies
   - Block only third-party cookies
   - Delete cookies already stored on your device
   - Receive notifications when a cookie is set

   Please note that disabling strictly necessary cookies may prevent you from accessing certain parts of the platform or using core B2B features such as logging in, posting RFQs, or managing vendor relationships.

4. Opt-Out Links
   For specific third-party analytics tools (such as Google Analytics), you can use their respective opt-out mechanisms.

Disabling cookies may impact your experience on SouthCaravan. Some features, such as remembering your login or saving RFQ preferences, may not work as intended.
