# SouthCaravan Public-Facing Site Structure

## Overview
Complete B2B vendor management platform public site including marketing, education, and community features.

## Public Pages

### 1. **Landing Page** (`/landing` or `/`)
- Hero section with value proposition
- Platform statistics (500+ vendors, $2B+ GMV, 10K+ buyers)
- Feature highlights (4 key benefits)
- How it works (3-step process)
- Testimonials from real users
- Call-to-action sections
- Comprehensive footer with links

### 2. **Features Page** (`/features`)
- Detailed feature explanations (8+ features)
- Role-based feature comparison (Buyers, Vendors, Admins)
- Advanced capabilities section
- Call-to-action to start free trial

### 3. **Pricing Page** (`/pricing`)
- **Buyer Plans:**
  - Starter (Free)
  - Professional ($299/mo)
  - Enterprise (Custom)
- **Vendor Plans:**
  - Starter (Free)
  - Growth ($99/mo)
  - Enterprise (2-3% commission)
- Commission structure breakdown
- FAQ section
- Free trial offer (14 days, no credit card)

### 4. **Vendor Directory** (`/public/vendors`)
- Searchable database of 500+ verified vendors
- Filter by category (manufacturing, distribution, wholesale, services, retail)
- Vendor cards with:
  - Rating and reviews
  - Product count
  - Years in business
  - Quick view profile button
- Mobile-responsive grid
- Statistics about platform scale

### 5. **Resources Page** (`/resources`)
- Featured article with expert content
- Grid of latest articles (6+ articles)
- Upcoming webinars section
- Free templates & tools (6 downloadable resources)
- Topics include:
  - Vendor evaluation
  - Procurement best practices
  - Digital transformation
  - Risk management
  - Negotiation tactics
  - Sustainability

### 6. **About Page** (`/about`)
- Company mission and vision
- Our story (founding and growth)
- Core values (4 key values)
- Leadership team (3+ founding team members)
- Company milestones timeline
- Key statistics
- Call-to-action

### 7. **Contact Page** (`/contact`)
- Contact form (name, email, company, subject, message)
- Contact information:
  - Email addresses
  - Phone number
  - Physical address
  - Business hours
- Quick help links
- FAQ section
- Multi-channel contact options

## Navigation
- **Sticky Public Navigation** visible on all public pages with:
  - SouthCaravan logo (clickable to home)
  - Navigation links: Features, Pricing, Vendors, Resources, About, Contact
  - Sign In and Get Started buttons
  - Mobile hamburger menu for responsive design
